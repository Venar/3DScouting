🏎️ Pinewood Derby: Tools & Jigs

Pinewood Derby Tracers

by AndyMac8

Templates that help scouts trace car outlines and key features directly onto their blocks of wood. Great for workshops.

Wheel Speed Chucks

by Alex

Mount standard BSA wheels into a hand drill or drill press for sanding and polishing. Achieves perfectly smooth, race-ready wheels.

Axle Insert Brace

by ninja_goatee

Jig to support the car body while inserting axles. Includes a version for longer wheelbase cars.

Axle Bender

by calebroylance

Tool for bending Pinewood Derby axles. Insert an extra axle in the side hole for leverage.

Inspection Box

by Old Gregg

Check if cars are within tolerance. Inner dimensions sized to the maximum allowable length and width per BSA rules.

Pinewood Derby Axle Armor

by RevHazlett

Screw-in covers for axle slots. Two sizes (tall 1.6mm and short 1mm). Protects axle slots from damage during handling and transport.

Pinewood Derby Trophies

⭐ Our Design — by the site curator

Complete modular trophy system for Cub Scout Pinewood Derby. Rank-specific badges (Tiger, Wolf, Bear, Webelos), silk+ metallic risers (gold/silver/bronze), optional weighted bases with tungsten pocket, real tire & wheel with customizable pack number. Pack-wide and den-level awards.
